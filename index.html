FAQ


How can I register for Knowledgeminds Academy?

If you do not have an account and you click a course, or you are on the login page, click the "Register" button and input your details. If successful, a success message is displayed.

What do I do after registering?

The next step after registration is to confirm your Email Address. Check your mailbox for an activation mail; click on the link to navigate you to the login page.

What's next after e-mail confirmation?

Once you have successfully logged in to the portal, the next thing to do is to update your profile.

How can I update my profile?

Click the picture icon on the dashboard and select "My Profile". Input your details and click the "Update" button to save.

How can I take a free course?

Click on "General Trainings" to view all courses or "Active Trainings" to view on-going trainings. Students can only join courses listed in the "Active Trainings" page.

How can I register interest for a course with no start date?

Click on "All Trainings" on the dashboard, select the course. If there is no start date, you will receive a message indicating your interest in email notification once the training is available. Click on "Yes". Once done, you will receive a mail immediately the course is available.

How can I find a trainings that I've joined?

Go to your "Dashboard" and select "My Trainings". This will list out all the trainings you have ever registered for.
